GENETIC CARS

What is a Genetic Algorithm?

Genetic algorithms (GAs) search for near-optimal solutions to a wide variety of problems with incomplete or imperfect information by emulating the process of natural selection. Applications of genetic algorithms include automated design, bioinformatics, economics, game theory, and training neural networks.

What is this program's purpose?

Presented here is an application of genetic algorithms. This program will randomly generate an initial car with a polygon body and two wheels randomly placed. Observe how the car evolves towards a more successful iteration to move farther and farther along the road.
